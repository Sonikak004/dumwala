About Dumwala

As avid foodies, we were always on the lookout for the perfect meal - one that not only tantalized our taste buds but also met our standards of hygiene and quality. However, we found ourselves disappointed, unable to find a biryani that truly satisfied our cravings for authentic flavors and cleanliness. Driven by our love for great food and dissatisfaction with the available options, we decided to take matters into our own hands. With love for biryani and a determination to create something great, we set out on a journey to craft the ultimate biryani. What started as a humble kitchen experiment soon blossomed into Dumwala. Our mission is simple yet profound: to bring together the richness of traditional recipes, the finest ingredients, and uncompromising hygiene standards to create biryani that not only delights the palate but also nourishes the soul.

Today, Dumwala Biryani stands as a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence. Every dish we serve tells the story of our passion, dedication, and relentless pursuit of culinary perfection. Join us on this flavorful journey and discover the magic of Dumwala for yourself. Whether you are craving the aromatic flavors of our classic Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, the zest of our Ambur Chicken Biryani, or the indulgent richness of our Special Mutton Biryani, we have something to tantalize every taste bud. Complemented by our array of delectable sides, a meal at Dumwala is an experience to savor. Step into our inviting ambiance, where the aroma of spices fills the air and the warmth of hospitality envelops you. At Dumwala, we don't just serve biryani; we serve memories and traditions, infused with the essence of culinary artistry. Come, join us, and let your senses embark on a journey of culinary delight.

The best service, for you.
